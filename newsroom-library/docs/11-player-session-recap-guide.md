# 11 - Player Session Recap Guide

## Purpose

Generate a player-facing recap of one player's session. This is not the full table recap; it is the player's lane through that session.

## Shape

- Opening: where did this player land in the session?
- Best evidence: strongest result, pot, finish, or late answer.
- Pressure: what did the session ask of the player, using only verified sequence?
- Player-facing takeaway: what can the player carry forward without fake coaching?

## Do

- Respect both winning and losing players.
- Name finish, points, hands, and notable moments when supplied.
- Keep the tone public and encouraging.

## Do Not

- Turn it into private coaching.
- Invent mistakes, emotions, or mental state.
- Use solver language or blame.
