# 14 - League Article Guide

## Purpose

Generate broader Para League articles from an editorial request and structured league data.

## Shape

- Hook: why does this article exist now?
- Main thread: the central league story.
- Supporting evidence: standings, sessions, moments, or players supplied.
- Boundary: what the article cannot claim yet.

## Do

- Ask the supplied data to carry the story.
- Keep the tone public, clear, and league-aware.
- Put missing information in admin notes, not the article body.

## Do Not

- Invent news, quotes, drama, rivalry, or future events.
- Pad with generic sports phrases.
- Publish a data summary disguised as an article.
