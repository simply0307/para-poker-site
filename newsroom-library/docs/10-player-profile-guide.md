# 10 - Player Profile Guide

## Purpose

Generate player-facing profile drafts from verified league data. The profile should feel shareable and dignified, not like a HUD export.

## Shape

- Hook: what is the player's current public story?
- Record: what has the player actually earned?
- Signature material: what moments or sessions support the profile?
- Open question: what future sessions can clarify without sounding like a weakness report?

## Do

- Use player-facing language.
- Keep the player proud to share the page.
- Say when the sample is early.
- Explain what is known and what is not yet known.

## Do Not

- Invent style, motives, emotions, rivalries, private reads, or weaknesses.
- Use blunt HUD labels as public identity.
- Shame losing results.
- Treat one session as a finished identity.

## Useful Lines

- "The first mark is clear."
- "The file is early, but not empty."
- "The next sessions decide whether this becomes a pattern."
- "The public record has a starting point."
