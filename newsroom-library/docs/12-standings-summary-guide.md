# 12 - Standings Summary Guide

## Purpose

Generate public standings summaries from approved standings data.

## Shape

- Hook: what does the table currently say?
- Top line: who leads and why, using supplied points/rank only.
- Chase line: who is close enough to matter?
- Boundary: what should not be overstated yet?

## Do

- Use plain league terms.
- Explain rank and points clearly.
- Keep the summary compact.

## Do Not

- Invent movement, streaks, clinches, or qualification stakes.
- Say "maximum points" unless supplied by league rules.
- Use audit language.
